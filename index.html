Isabelle

Isabelle is a yellow Shih Tzu dog with floppy ears and a ponytail. Her hair is tied up with a ribbon that has a bell on top. As such, whenever she walks, a little jingling sound is made.

Isabelle is the mayor's highly capable secretary. She can be forgetful sometimes, but you can always count on her for information about the town, and she's happy to cheer you on in all your mayor-ly duties.
